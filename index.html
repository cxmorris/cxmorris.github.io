About me

I am a freshman intending to study computer science at Harvard University. I am currently involved with Harvard College in Asia Program and also worked as a research fellow this past summer in the Earth and Planetary Sciences department. Once the school year starts, I plan to get involved with Harvard Computing Society, Harvard Open Data Project, and Women in Computer Science. One of my passions is to mentor and helper younger peers. I have previously worked as an intern counselor with Girls Rock Math and currently work with the Pacific Science Center summer camps to educate elementary students in STEM related curriculums. I have also been tutoring privately for about 3 years.
